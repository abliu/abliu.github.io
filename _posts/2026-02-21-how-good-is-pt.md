Having undergone physical therapy (PT) for shoulder issues, and seen many volleyball and office worker friends go through PT, I wanted to see what the medical literature says about the quantitative benefits of PT and related interventions. Some questions I had:

- How much of recovery is due to the passage of time versus the intervention itself?
- How often do people see a full recovery after injury?
- How long does recovery take?

I found existing literature reviews and studies, and excerpted the key figures from each. Consider this a "visual literature review!"

Basic details: what intervention/control and who (including age and severity), interpreting the score. less important: when (usually captured in figure) and where.

Maybe we just put all the figures on one graph for now?
